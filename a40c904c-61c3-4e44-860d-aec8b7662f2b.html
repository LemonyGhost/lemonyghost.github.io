Description

In a world ruled by vibrant frog characters, the Kingdom of Callidrya beckons. You play as Rill, a frog prince on a quest to find his lost sister in the midst of a royal conspiracy.

Use your tongue to swing, slap and grapple your way through combat, puzzles and platforming. Leap, climb, and swim with the agility only a frog can master. 

Your wisp companion, Lumi, channels spirit magic to aid in combat, solve puzzles, and scout ahead. Explore forsaken temples to unlock the power of the Great Spirits and enhance Lumi’s abilities.

Explore a frog’s world filled with diverse, amphibious characters and unique environments, where your tongue can manipulate any part of your surroundings. But not all are friendly; use your tongue to stun, disarm, or even swallow foes.

The characters and world of Frogreign are brought to life in a refined pixel art style, with frame-by-frame animation, beautiful fantasy environments and evocative 2D lighting.

SNES-inspired musical score by Alejandro Pérez Heinze, a composer for Kena: Bridge of Spirits who has worked alongside the composers that brought you the recognisable soundtrack of 'Journey'.


  • Swing and grapple with your frog tongue from all surfaces, even enemies.

  • Utilise free-flowing frog movement abilities to traverse with speed and style.

  • Explore an inter-connected fantasy world of amphibious creatures.

  • Light the path ahead & unleash ancient magic with your spirit companion, Lumi.

  • Unlock over a dozen abilities to empower you in combat and traversal.

  • Equip artefacts and equipment to create a unique loadout for your adventure.

  • Delve into abandoned temples filled with peril and prizes in equal measure.
